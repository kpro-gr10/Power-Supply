Instructions

In the little town of Ohmville, electricity is in high demand. As director of the sole electric utility, it is your job to make sure all inhabitants are able to work their electric coffee machines and play stimulating games on their mobile phones. You have at your disposal a certain amount of money, as well as the ability to build power plants and lay cables. You are the Watcher in the Night, the Bringer of Power and the Omnipotent Ottoman.

During the course of the game, buildings will appear scattered around the map. In order to keep the locals happy, and to keep money flowing into your pocket, you need to build power plants and connect the buildings and power plants through power lines. If a building is not connected to a power plant within a certain time frame, its inhabitants will move to another city and your health bar will take a blow.

A powerplant can only serve so many buildings. When a power plant has reached maximum capacity, you need to build more power plants or upgrade your existing ones. Tap on a power plant to see its upgrade options.

Be mindful of your health! If the health bar runs out of juice, it's Game Over for you!

Game elements

Powerplant: is the building that can serve power to buildings.
Powerlines: is the connection between houses and powerstations.
Building mode: when building powerstations or powercables, you will enter building mode.
Coin: when a building is connected to a powerplant, it starts producing money. If a coin is showing on a building, you can collect the money.

Buildings

Small size house: require 1 unit of power
Medium size house: require 2 units of power
Big house: require 3 units of power
Very big house: require 4 units of power
Factory building: require 5 units of power
Company building: require 6 units of power
